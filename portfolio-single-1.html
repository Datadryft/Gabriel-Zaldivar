Divvy Trips

Project Date March 9th, 2024
Role Bike Share, Case Study
Client unslate
Visit Tableau

This is a Bike Share Case Study that I had the privilage to work on. The Main goal was to find out what the difference between the customers (one time users) vs Monthly Suscribers, with the purpose of seeking more profitable advertising/marketing

I was able to quantify the objective to 4 catagories what is the age distribution? What is the Customer to Sub ratio? who uses it more frequently (Customers or Subscribers) and when is it used more frequently? And last but not least what is the average ride length
